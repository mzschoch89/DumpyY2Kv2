# Match the Y2K Design Screenshot

## Overview
Redesign the app to closely match the screenshot design — updating colors, card styles, typography, tab structure, and screen layouts.

---

### **Tab Bar Changes**
- 4 tabs: **Gym** (home icon), **Plans** (calendar icon), **Gains** (chart icon), **Social** (people icon)
- "Exercises" tab removed — exercise list still accessible from within screens
- Social tab shows a beautiful "Coming Soon" placeholder

---

### **Visual Design Overhaul**
- **Dashed pink borders** made more prominent on cards (matching screenshot)
- **Green/lime outlined "START WORKOUT" button** instead of pink filled
- **Italic accent text** on select headers (e.g. "Glute *Gains!*", "YOUR *Progress*") for the Y2K editorial vibe
- **Mesh gradient cards** kept but refined to better match the pastel pink/green/lavender blends in the screenshot
- Bolder, more condensed typography for section headers
- More spacing and breathing room between sections

---

### **Gym Screen (Home)**
- Header: "GLUTE *Gains!* 🍑" with italic accent styling
- Large workout card showing the current workout name with subtitle details (effort level, phase info)
- Green outlined **"START WORKOUT"** button
- **"RECENTLY ADDED"** section with exercise cards below (sets, reps, category info)
- Removed the quick-nav buttons (EXERCISES, PROGRAMS, etc.)
- Stats row and streak card kept with refined styling

---

### **Plans Screen**
- Kept as-is with design refinements: bolder gradient cards, level badges (like "BEGINNER", "INTERMEDIATE"), dashed borders
- Warmup card and nutrition tips preserved

### **Gains Screen (Progress)**
